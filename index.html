GAME RULES: Click to know

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls two dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1 in any of the dices, all his ROUND score gets lost and the next player gets the turn
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score, but doing so gives the turn to the the next player.
  • The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game or the players can choose the maximum score to win.
